About Us

Nahjul Huda Dars is a dedicated Islamic institution focused on spreading the messages and teachings of the Quran and Sunnah through the paths of Samastha Kerala Jem-iyyathul Ulama. Our mission is to inspire hearts with wisdom and educate minds with moral values, and build a perfect person and community rooted in faith and unity
